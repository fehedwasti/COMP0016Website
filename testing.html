Testing

Unit Testing



Upon arriving at a stage where we had a working prototype, testing had become a vital part of further developing the game. As a result, we wrote several different unit tests to ensure all aspects of the skeletal tracking were behaving as expected.

Prototype Implementation


Currently, we have developed a working prototype of the skeletal tracker and implemented it into a boxing game. The skeletal tracker is mapping movements of the arms and upper body and if a certain gesture such as punching with your right hand is detected, the action is recorded by the boxing game. However, sometimes the tracker fails to detect the motion and so the action is not registered.


The next stages of the project will be to fix the unregistered actions bug, add more actions such as dodging objects and allowing the user to increase the difficulty, the speed of the objects. Furthermore, a scoreboard system could be used to allow users to track their performance progess to see which areas of practice need improvement.
