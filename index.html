Training

Training for both professionals all the way down to amateurs, now available at your home!

Abstract

This project uses the new developing technology of mixed reality, through the Microsoft Hololens, as well as the Windows v2 Kinect to develop a software that uses skeletal tracking to help train users in a particular sport through a mixed reality environment. The particular sports we chose to do for this project are boxing and golf. In boxing for example the user will train their accuracy by hitting a series of incoming 3D targets, and the software will detect the parts of the target hit by the user, and at the end give them a score depending on how close to the center they hit. What this software is aiming to achieve is making the availability of training as easy and convenient as possible for all users, by not having to go to a boxing ring or golf course or not have to set equipment up in order to begin a simple training regimen. Instead this becomes easily available at the comfort of home.
